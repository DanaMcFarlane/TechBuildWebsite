What To Do?

Here, you will find our absolute favorite things to do in and around Downtown Hartford. With everything closed in the evenings and on weekends, this guide might help you out when you just cannot watch Netflix any longer. These activities range from nightlife to family time, so we hope this will help you find something to enjoy!

Visit TripAdvisor Visit Groupon

City Steam Brewery is a popular spot for all people that live and work in Hartford. While you have to be of age to enjoy a nice cocktail, there is good food as well. It provides a good source of nightlife for people looking for a night out in this small city.

Visit City Steam Brewery
$$-$$$

Hartford is located near the Connecticut River. Because of this, you can spend the day on the river boating, having a picnic near the water, or fishing with friends. It is a great weekend activity especially during the beautiful summer months.

Visit the Riverfront
FREE

Hartford's Spotlight Theater is another option when looking for something to do. Here you can catch a movie with friends and eat some tasty movie theater popcorn. Spotlight Theater is located on Front Street and plays all the recent movies that have been released.

Visit Spotlight Theater
$$
