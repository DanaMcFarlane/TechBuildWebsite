Ready to Eat?

Here, you will find our absolute favorite places to eat in Downtown Hartford. We have included the price range for each spot as well as other restuarants that are nearby that did not make our top 3, but you might also enjoy!

Visit TripAdvisor Visit Yelp

Bear's Smokehouse BBQ is located in Hartford as well as other locations around Connecticut. Bear's specializes in barbequed food and is a popular destination for people that live and work in Hartford. From tasty salads to delcious sandwiches, your options aren't limited at Bear's!


Visit Bear's
$$

Ted's Montana Grill is another popular restaurant in Hartford that is located on Front Street near the Travelers campus. Ted's specializes in bison meat, but there are also other meat options like turkey, beef, or chicken. Ted's is a chain restaurant throughout the US and Connecticut. Stop by Ted's for some good food and good times!

Visit Ted's
$$

The Blind Pig is a popular pizzeria in Hartford. While you aren't in New York City for some classic New York pizza, this is your next best thing! Pizzas are served with delicius cocktails to make this the classiest pizza you'll eat in Hartford.




Visit The Blind Pig
$
